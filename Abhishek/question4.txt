Question No.04

To delete one commit or multiple commits permanently following are the stpes-

1.To Delete last/Top commit Permanantly-
  'git reset --hard HEAD~1'

2.To Delete any One Particular commit Permanantly having position 'n' -
  'git reset --hard HEAD~n'

3.To Delete any Many commits from top Permanantly -
  'git reset --hard HEAD~COUNT'

4.To Delete any Particular Commit Permanantly- 
  'git revert <commit_ID> <commit_ID>'
